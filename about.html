About Our Ministry

Our Vision

Restoring hope and dignity through God's provision

Our vision is to restore hope and dignity to the most vulnerable members of society through the compassionate provision of God's love. We envision a world where widows and orphans experience security, love, and opportunity through our ministry's dedicated service.

Mtazamo wetu ni kurejesha tumaini na utukufu kwa washirika wa chini kabisa wa jamii kupitia upako wa upendo wa Mungu. Tunawaza ulimwengu ambapo mjane na yatima watajiona salama, na upendo, na fursa kupitia huduma ya kina cha shirika letu.

Notre vision est de restaurer l'espérance et la dignité des membres les plus vulnérables de la société grâce à la disposition compatissante de l'amour de Dieu. Nous imaginons un monde où les veuves et les orphelins connaissent la sécurité, l'amour et l'opportunité grâce au service dévoué de notre ministère.

Our Mission

Showing God's love through care, compassion and empowerment

Our mission is to demonstrate God's unconditional love by providing holistic care, compassion, and empowerment to widows and orphans. Through our dedicated service, we aim to meet both physical and spiritual needs, offering sustainable solutions that honor God and transform lives.

Misioni yetu ni kuonyesha upendo usio na masharti wa Mungu kwa kutoa utunzaji kamili, huruma, na uwezo wa kuwezesha mjane na yatima. Kupitia huduma yetu iliyojitoa, tunamaanisha kukutana na mahitaji ya mwili na roho, na kutoa suluhisho ambazo zinamtukuza Mungu na kubadilisha maisha.

Notre mission est de démontrer l'amour inconditionnel de Dieu en prodiguant des soins complets, de la compassion et de l'autonomisation aux veuves et aux orphelins. Par notre service dévoué, nous visons à répondre aux besoins physiques et spirituels, en offrant des solutions durables qui honorent Dieu et transforment les vies.

Our Purpose

Serving God by caring for the vulnerable

Our purpose is to serve God by caring for the most vulnerable members of society. Guided by the Holy Spirit, we are committed to addressing the critical needs of widows and orphans, providing them with resources, support, and spiritual guidance that reflect the heart of God.

Kusudi letu ni kutumikia Mungu kwa kuwatunza washirika wa chini kabisa wa jamii. Tukiongozwa na Roho Mtakatifu, tunaazimia kujibu mahitaji muhimu ya mjane na yatima, kuwapa rasilimali, usaidizi, na mwelekeo wa kiroho ambao unaonyesha moyo wa Mungu.

Notre objectif est de servir Dieu en prenant soin des membres les plus vulnérables de la société. Guidés par le Saint-Esprit, nous nous engageons à répondre aux besoins critiques des veuves et des orphelins, en leur fournissant des ressources, du soutien et un guide spirituel qui reflètent le cœur de Dieu.
